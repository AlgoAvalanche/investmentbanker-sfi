# Research Notes: M&A Process Walkthrough - Pitch to Closing

## Sources Verified

### 1. PwC Global M&A Industry Trends (2026 Outlook)
URL: https://www.pwc.com/gx/en/services/deals/trends.html
Status: Fetched and verified

Key data points:
- 2025 global M&A deal values increased 36% year-over-year
- Deal volumes rose only 1% in 2025 vs 2024
- Megadeals (>$5bn) surged from 63 in 2024 to 111 in 2025
- Americas: deal values up 55%, representing 60% of global deal value
- EMEA: deal values up 19%; APAC up 10%
- Technology sector led megadeals with 26 in 2025
- Approximately one-third of the 100 largest 2025 corporate transactions cited AI as strategic rationale
- 2026 outlook: value growth expected to remain elevated even as volumes stay muted

### 2. Axial M&A Fee Guide 2024-2025
URL: https://www.axial.net/forum/ma-fee-guide-2024-2025/
Status: Fetched and verified

Key data points:
- Lehman Formula: used by 44% of firms
- Accelerator Formula: 20% of firms (up from 16% in 2023)
- Flat-fee structure: 26% of firms (down from 31%)
- 30% of surveyed firms raised at least one fee type in 2024
- Survey covered 150+ Axial members and mid-market dealmakers
- Most advisors maintained unchanged fee structures in 2024; modest increases expected in 2025

### 3. Search Data - M&A Advisor Fees (RoseBiz, MNA Community, First Page Sage)
URLs: https://rosebiz.com/what-are-m-a-advisor-fees-in-2024-2025/ | https://mnacommunity.com/insights/ma-fees-by-deal-size/ | https://firstpagesage.com/business/ma-advisory-fee-structure/
Status: Search results verified

Key data points:
- Average success fee: ~5.5% for a $5M deal, ~2.1% for a $100M deal
- Deals above $100M: typically 1-2% sell-side success fee
- Mid-market ($10-30M EBITDA range): typically 3-5% success fee
- Retainer fees: $50,000-$250,000 upfront, sometimes structured as monthly payments over 4-12 months

### 4. HSR Antitrust Data - ComplexDiscovery
URL: https://complexdiscovery.com/second-requests-settle-in-hsr-data-points-to-a-new-normal-in-ma-scrutiny/
Status: Fetched and verified

Key data points:
- FY 2024: 59 Second Requests issued across 1,973 adjusted transactions = 3.0% overall rate
- FTC issued 30; DOJ issued 29
- Mega-deals (>$10B): nearly 50% received Second Requests
- Mid-range ($1-10B): approximately 6.4% Second Request rate
- Sub-billion dollar transactions: significantly lower rates
- New HSR rules effective February 10, 2025 require substantially expanded upfront documentation
- FY 2024 total filings: 2,031 transactions; FY 2025: 2,101 transactions
- Of 59 FY2024 Second Requests: 32 faced enforcement actions
- Second Request compliance typically takes 2-4 months, can cost millions, may extend close by 10 months+

### 5. RWI/Rep and Warranty Insurance - CBIZ
URL: https://www.cbiz.com/insights/article/representations-and-warranties-insurance-in-2025-ma-trends-and-best-practices
Status: Fetched and verified

Key data points:
- RWI premium rates dropped from ~5% of policy limits (early 2022) to 2.5-3% (2025)
- RWI used on 20-30% of secondary-led PE deals
- ABA Private Target M&A Deal Points Study: 55% of private transactions used RWI in 2023, down from record 65% in 2021
- Financial reps, customer/contracts, and compliance drive approximately 78% of all RWI claims
- Coverage now available for deals as small as $25M
- RWI market slowest by U.S. dealmaking volume in 20+ years at start of 2025

### 6. M&A Failure / Due Diligence Statistics (Acquisition Stars, CFA Institute, Bain)
URLs: https://acquisitionstars.com/ma-failure-rate | https://blogs.cfainstitute.org/investor/2025/02/03/whats-the-winning-ingredient-in-ma-the-answer-lies-in-due-diligence/
Status: Search results verified

Key data points:
- 70-90% of M&A transactions fail to create expected value (widely cited)
- Top three failure reasons: overpaying for target (42%), inadequate due diligence (31%), poor post-merger integration (27%)
- Nearly 60% of executives attributed deal failure to poor due diligence
- Deals with 90+ days of due diligence have 34% higher success rates than those with less than 45 days
- Bayer's $63B Monsanto acquisition has erased $50B+ in shareholder value
