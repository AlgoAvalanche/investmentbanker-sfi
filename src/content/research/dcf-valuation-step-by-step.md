### 2. Wall Street Prep - Terminal Growth Rate
URL: https://www.wallstreetprep.com/knowledge/terminal-growth-rate/
VERIFIED: Yes - page fetched successfully.

Key data:
- Terminal growth rate range: 2.0% to 4.0%, averaging ~3.0% in practice
- Terminal value can comprise "around three-quarters" of total intrinsic value
- Example: Year 5 FCF of $74M, 2.5% growth rate, 8.0% WACC = $1.38B terminal value
- Growth rate must not exceed WACC (mathematical constraint)
- Anchored to GDP growth to maintain credibility

### 3. Wall Street Prep - Terminal Value (DCF)
URL: https://www.wallstreetprep.com/knowledge/terminal-value/
VERIFIED: Yes - page fetched successfully.

Key data:
- Exit multiple approach "viewed more favorably" because assumptions are more defensible
- 8.0x EBITDA example used
- Gordon Growth formula: TV = [Final Year FCF x (1 + g)] / (r - g)
- PV of TV example: $305M out of $432M total EV (70.6% of total)

### 4. Macabacus - DCF Terminal Value
URL: https://macabacus.com/valuation/dcf-terminal-value
VERIFIED: Yes - page fetched successfully.

Key data:
- 5-year DCF: terminal value = approximately 75% of total enterprise value
- 10-year DCF: terminal value = approximately 50% of total enterprise value
- Perpetuity growth rate range anchored to inflation (2-3%) and GDP growth (4-5%)
- Perpetuity method "not used as frequently in practice"

### 5. Street of Walls - DCF Analysis
URL: https://www.streetofwalls.com/finance-training-courses/investment-banking-technical-training/discounted-cash-flow-analysis/
VERIFIED: Yes - page fetched successfully.

Key data:
- Typical projection period: 5 to 10 years
- Terminal multiple example: EV/EBITDA range of 6.0x to 8.0x
- Perpetuity method example: 1% growth, 11% WACC (conservative)
- FCF projections more sensitive to value than discount rates

### 6. Wall Street Prep - WACC Guide
URL: https://www.wallstreetprep.com/knowledge/wacc/
VERIFIED: Yes - page fetched successfully.

Key data:
- Equity value for public companies: diluted shares x share price
- Effective vs. marginal tax rate distinction (21% federal + state/local)
- Cost of debt: YTM on bonds for public companies, synthetic rating for unrated
- Standard formula components confirmed

### 7. Mergers & Inquisitions - DCF Model
URL: https://mergersandinquisitions.com/dcf-model/
VERIFIED: Yes - page fetched successfully.

Key data:
- Projection periods: 5, 10, 15, or 20+ years depending on company lifecycle
- Walmart DCF example: WACC approx. 4.0-4.37% (~90% equity, ~10% debt)
- Terminal growth must stay below long-term GDP growth rate
- CapEx should exceed D&A when cash flows are growing
